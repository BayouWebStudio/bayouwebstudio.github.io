The Artist

Geometry in Ink

Eric Hare is a geometric tattoo artist based at The Tattoo Temple in San Diego, CA. With a mastery of precision line work, mandala compositions, and sacred geometry, Eric creates tattoos that live on the boundary between mathematics and art.

His work spans full sleeves to intricate chest pieces — each design a custom exploration of symmetry, dotwork, and organic pattern. Whether you're drawn to strict geometric structures or flowing mandalas, Eric collaborates closely to bring your vision to life.

Every piece is designed for your body, your story, and built to last a lifetime.
