About the Artist

Geometry
as Art

Elias Jeis is a geometric tattoo artist based in Santiago, Chile, with a reputation for creating deeply intricate, mathematically precise designs that transcend skin — they become sacred objects.

His work spans full sleeves, back pieces, and standalone mandalas, each built with obsessive detail through dotwork, linework, and blackwork techniques rooted in sacred geometry.

With over 80K followers on Instagram and clients traveling from across South America and Europe, Elias brings the same precision to every session — regardless of scale.
