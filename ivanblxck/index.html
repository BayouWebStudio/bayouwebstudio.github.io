The Art of Darkness

Ivan Escobar is a Denver-based tattoo artist known for his mastery of blackwork, geometric patterns, and ornamental mandala designs. With over a decade of dedication to his craft, Ivan has cultivated a signature style that merges mathematical precision with organic beauty.

Each piece is a meditation — intricate dotwork and sacred geometry woven into permanent marks that carry meaning beyond aesthetics. Based in Denver, CO, with a global clientele drawn by his 145K+ Instagram following.
