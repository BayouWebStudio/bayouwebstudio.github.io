Artist Spotlight: Alan Gutiérrez — Where Film, Music, and Ink Meet

By GetMinted · March 9, 2026

Some tattoo artists follow trends. Alan Gutiérrez doesn't follow anything — he writes his own story. Known online as @alangutierrez, Alan is a 5-star rated tattoo artist whose creative vision pulls from an unusual well: film, music, and the visual language of cinema. The result is tattoo work that doesn't just sit on skin — it moves, it breathes, it means something.

His portfolio site, guccierrezz.com, is powered by GetMinted — and it's a clean reflection of exactly who he is as an artist.

About the Artist

Alan Gutiérrez is a visionary tattoo artist currently working as a guest artist across studios, with a strong presence in the Los Angeles area. He takes appointments by request and books through Instagram DM — a testament to how much of his client base is built on genuine connection and organic reach.

What makes Alan different is where he draws his inspiration. While most tattoo artists reference other tattoo artists, Alan's creative inputs are film scores, live performances, and cinematic storytelling. He's also a filmmaker — actively promoting a short film called Síncopa — and a composer. For Alan, tattooing isn't a separate discipline. It's another medium.

His Style

Alan specializes in illustrative and fine-line blackwork, with a particular love for pieces that incorporate elements of nature and surrealism. His work has the kind of quiet confidence that comes from an artist who isn't trying to make something flashy — he's trying to make something that lasts.

  • Illustrative blackwork — rich contrast, storytelling line weight
  • Fine line — precision work with a delicate, graphic sensibility
  • Nature & surrealism — organic forms with dreamlike composition
  • Film & music-inspired — themes, mood, rhythm translated into skin

His custom work thrives when clients give him creative latitude. Alan loves projects that blend personal meaning with artistic freedom — the kind of commission where the client says "I trust you" and means it.

His Work

Every piece in Alan's portfolio carries a sense of intention. There's no filler. The linework is confident and deliberate, the compositions feel considered rather than assembled. Whether he's working a botanical piece with surrealist overtones or a fine-line portrait with cinematic stillness, the through-line is always the same: this was made for someone specific, by someone who cared.

Clients describe his work as "unlike anything I've ever seen" — and the 5-star reputation backs that up. He's the kind of artist where the waitlist isn't a barrier; it's part of the experience.

"The visuals and rhythms of film and music often inspire my tattoo work, influencing the flow and storytelling aspects of my designs. I love incorporating those themes into custom pieces."
