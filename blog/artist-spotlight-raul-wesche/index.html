Meet the Artist Behind GetMinted: Raul Wesche

By GetMinted · March 9, 2026

Most software is built by people who've never done the job it's meant to support. GetMinted is different. It was built by a working tattoo artist — someone who spent years watching talented people lose clients to bad websites, slow booking systems, and zero Google presence. Someone who got tired of waiting for a solution and built one himself.

That artist is Raul Wesche. And his story is the reason GetMinted exists.

About the Artist

Raul Wesche is a Houston-based tattoo artist with 12 years of professional experience and 297,000 Instagram followers (@weschetattoo). He owns and operates Tattoo Temple Houston, where he works five days a week by appointment only. His specialty — large-scale geometric and sacred geometry tattoos — has taken him from Houston to Mexico, Belize, and tattoo conventions across the country.

He's not an influencer who happens to tattoo. He's a craftsman who happened to build an audience by being very, very good at his craft.

His Style

Geometric tattooing is one of the most technically demanding disciplines in the industry. Lines have to be exact. Patterns have to be mathematically consistent across curved surfaces. There's no hiding a mistake inside organic shading — every error is visible, permanent, and measured in fractions of a millimeter.

Raul has spent 12 years mastering this. His large-scale geometric and sacred geometry pieces are characterized by:

  • Precise linework — clean, confident, unforgiving geometry
  • Sacred geometry — Flower of Life, Metatron's Cube, mandalic structures
  • Large-scale compositions — full sleeves, back pieces, chest panels
  • Mathematical harmony — patterns that work with the body's natural curves

At his rate, clients plan. They save. They wait. And they come back. That's the mark of an artist whose work holds its value for life — on skin and in reputation.

His Work

With nearly 300,000 followers on Instagram, Raul's audience isn't a vanity metric — it's a decade of consistently posting work that people actually want to look at. His feed is a masterclass in geometric composition: repeating forms that shouldn't be beautiful somehow always are, patterns that look impossible until you realize the hands that drew them have been doing this since 2013.

He's tattooed clients internationally, built a studio that runs on reputation alone, and created machines under his own brand, Wesche Machines. He's not just an artist — he's an operator who understands every part of the business from needle to invoice.

"I built GetMinted because I needed it. I was watching artists around me lose clients to Instagram outages, bad websites, and zero Google presence. The problem was obvious. Nobody was solving it for us. So we did."

Why He Built GetMinted

Here's the honest truth about the tattoo industry's online problem: Instagram is rented land. You can have 300K followers and lose them overnight to an algorithm change, a policy violation, or a platform ban. Your DMs are not a booking system. Your link-in-bio is not a website.

Raul knew this firsthand. As a high-volume artist with a premium clientele, he needed a professional web presence that could rank on Google, handle inquiries, and represent his work at the level it deserved. The options available were either too expensive, too generic, or too slow to build. So he built GetMinted — a platform that creates professional tattoo artist websites in minutes, with SEO built in from day one.

The first GetMinted site he launched was his own. getminted.ai/weschetattoo is the proof of concept. If it works for a 297K-follower geometric artist running a Houston studio — it works.
