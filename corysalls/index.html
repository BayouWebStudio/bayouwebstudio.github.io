About Cory

Cory Salls is a Houston-based tattoo artist operating out of Gold Rush Collective — one of Houston's premier tattoo shops. With a dedicated following of 24,000+ across Instagram and a portfolio spanning hundreds of pieces, Cory's work speaks for itself.

Every piece is custom-designed from scratch — no flash, no shortcuts. Whether it's a bold statement sleeve or an intricate fine-line piece, Cory brings the same level of care and precision to every client.

Deposits and transactions are non-refundable. Cory's time is valuable — and so is yours. Come prepared, come committed, and leave with art you'll carry for life.
