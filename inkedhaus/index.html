The Healing System

Four stages.
One complete protocol.

Most aftercare is an afterthought. InkedHaus engineered a complete healing protocol — each product formulated for a specific stage of your tattoo's recovery.

Stage 01

Wash

Gentle, antimicrobial cleanse. Removes plasma and bacteria without stripping the skin barrier. Start here, every time.

Stage 02

Soothe

Botanical-infused balm that calms inflammation and locks in moisture during the most critical healing window — days 1–5.

Stage 03

Restore

Lightweight lotion accelerates skin regeneration and minimizes peeling. Keeps color vivid as the skin re-seals.

Stage 04

Maintain

Daily moisturizer with UV defense. Because healed ink still needs protection. Use it forever.
