About the Artist

Precision. Pattern. Purpose.

Joey Kintz is a Sydney-based tattoo artist specialising in geometric, blackwork, symmetry, and ornamental tattooing. Based at Whistler Street Tattoo, Joey brings mathematical precision and artistic intuition to every piece.

With a philosophy rooted in balance and structure, Joey's work draws on sacred geometry, intricate dotwork, and bold blackwork — creating tattoos that are as precise as they are striking.

Currently taking bookings through Instagram and the studio website. Custom designs and flash available.
