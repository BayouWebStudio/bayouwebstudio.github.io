About Kodi

Dark Lines.
Anime Soul.

Kodi Ellis is a black & grey and anime/illustrative tattoo specialist based in Indianapolis, Indiana. Known for his deeply detailed anime portraits, manga-style character work, and dark neo-traditional illustrative pieces, Kodi brings the world of animation to life permanently on skin.

With over 163K followers and a reputation built on precision and passion for the craft, Kodi is one of the premier anime tattoo artists in the Midwest — and his books are open.

As owner of The Grand Line Tattoo in Indianapolis — a shop named after One Piece's legendary sea route — Kodi has built a space where anime culture and tattooing collide.
