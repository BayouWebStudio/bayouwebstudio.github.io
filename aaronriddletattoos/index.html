About the Artist

AZ's Best
Tattooer

Aaron Riddle is Phoenix's most vibrant tattoo artist — a master of color realism, Japanese traditional, manga-inspired, and psychedelic neo-traditional work. His tattoos don't just sit on skin; they come alive with explosive color and razor-sharp detail.

Based at Desert Psychedelic Studio in Phoenix, Aaron brings boundless energy and technical mastery to every piece. With 44K followers and a reputation as AZ's finest, his books fill fast — and for good reason.
