About

Crafting Realism in Every Detail

Based in Houston, Texas, John Paul Roldan is a verified realism specialist and co-owner of Demon Ink Tattoo. With a refined eye for detail and texture, John Paul brings portraits, animals, and surreal imagery to life on skin — in both black & grey and vivid color. Every piece is built to last and built to impress.
