About the Artist

Froy Vargas

Jonatan "Froy" Vargas is a multidisciplinary visual artist whose work explores the liminal space between imagination and reality. Through painting, illustration, and fine art, he builds worlds that feel simultaneously familiar and otherworldly.

His work channels a deep sense of narrative — each piece an invitation into a scene, a moment, a feeling that outlasts the canvas. Froy's practice is driven by curiosity, craft, and an unrelenting commitment to bringing the invisible to light.
