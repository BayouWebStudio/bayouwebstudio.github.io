About Mike

Biology Grad Turned
Global Adventurer

Mike Corey traded a biology degree and five years of backpacking into one of the most-watched adventure travel channels on the planet. From swimming with whale sharks in the Philippines to eating the world's most dangerous foods, his content doesn't just show places — it dares you to experience them.

An Emmy Award winner for Outstanding Host on Uncharted Adventure (The Weather Channel) and a BBC television presenter, Mike has turned raw curiosity into a career that spans YouTube, network TV, podcasting, and ocean conservation research. His podcast Against the Odds earned an Ambie nomination, proving his storytelling works in any medium.

His mission is simple and relentless: help people conquer their fears so they can see the world for themselves.
