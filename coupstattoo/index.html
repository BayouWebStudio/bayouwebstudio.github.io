The Artist

Precision in Every Dot

Liam Couper is a Scotland-based tattoo artist specialising in dotwork and geometric tattooing — intricate, mathematically precise designs that transform skin into living geometry.

Based at @aeternustattoocompany, Liam has built a global following through award-winning work that pushes the boundaries of what's possible with a single needle. His pieces range from delicate dotwork mandalas to full-scale ornamental back pieces.

An internationally recognised competitor, Liam has taken home top honours at conventions including BEST ORNAMENTAL and BEST DECORATIVE awards across the UK tattoo scene.

Specialties

What I Do Best

⬡

Geometric

Precise, symmetrical geometric forms — from sacred geometry to complex tessellations — built with mathematical precision on any body placement.

✦

Dotwork

Thousands of individual dots creating depth, gradient, and texture. A meditative craft that rewards both the artist and the wearer.

◎

Mandalas

From elbow mandalas to full back mandalas — intricate radial designs that flow with the body's natural curves and proportions.

❋

Ornamental

Rich ornamental compositions with floral, baroque, and decorative elements — award-winning work that speaks for itself.

◈

Large Scale

Full back pieces, leg sleeves, chest and stomach projects — large-scale work that tells a complete visual story across the entire canvas.

🏆

Award Winning

Best Ornamental, Best Decorative — recognised by judges at top UK tattoo conventions for technical mastery and artistic vision.
