About Krime Image Tattoos

Houston's Owner-Operated Custom Tattoo Shop

Krime is more than an artist — he's the owner and creative force behind Krime Image Tattoos, an established custom tattoo shop located in the heart of North Houston.

With a reputation built on precision, creativity, and client-first service, Krime has created a shop environment where artistry meets professionalism. Every client who walks through the door receives a personalized experience — from initial consultation to the final result.

Whether you're looking for a full sleeve, a bold statement piece, or your first tattoo, Krime Image Tattoos delivers work that stands the test of time — and the eye of any critic.
