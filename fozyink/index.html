Fozy is a Hong Kong-based tattoo artist renowned for precise blackwork and flowing geometric patterns. Working out of Soul Free Studio (@soulfree.studio), Fozy has built an international reputation for large-scale body suits that push the boundary between art and skin.

With a meticulous approach to negative space and pattern flow, each piece is custom-designed to honor the body's natural movement and anatomy. Fozy regularly travels globally ‚Äî joining world-class conventions in Italy, the Netherlands, Germany, the US, and Southeast Asia.
