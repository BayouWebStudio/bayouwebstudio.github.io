About Chase Nolan

PRECISE.
INTENTIONAL.

Chase Nolan is a verified traveling tattoo artist with 29.9K followers, known for intricate geometric blackwork, dotwork patterns, and large-scale body suit projects. Based out of Sacred Mind Tattoo (@sacredmindstattoo), Chase tours the United States year-round.

Whether it's a geometric leg sleeve, a full body takeover, or a precise chest piece — every tattoo is built with intentional line work and architectural precision. Chase's work speaks for itself on the skin.
