About the Artist

Chase Nolan

Chase Nolan is a verified color realism and portrait tattoo artist known for creating bold, hyper-detailed, and emotionally charged large-scale tattoos. Based out of Sacred Mind Tattoo (@sacredmindstattoo), Chase takes his artistry on the road — traveling city to city, bringing world-class work directly to clients across the US.

With nearly 30K followers on Instagram and a YouTube presence, Chase's work speaks for itself: vivid color, flawless transitions, and portraits that feel alive on skin.
