# Britt's Body Art — BWS Demo Site

**Artist:** Britt (@brittsbodyart)  
**Location:** Cypress, Houston TX — Revenge Tattoo Studio  
**Demo URL:** https://bayouwebstudio.com/brittsbodyart/  
**Built:** 2025 by Bayou Web Studios

---

## Strategy

This is a **personal brand site** — distinct from Revenge Tattoo Studio's shop website. The positioning:

> "Shop has a website. *She* needs her own brand."

When Britt moves studios, her personal site/brand stays with her. Her bookings, her portfolio, her identity.

---

## Design

- **Theme:** Deep charcoal + rose gold + warm cream
- **Typography:** Cormorant Garamond (logo) + Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body)
- **Vibe:** Premium, feminine-leaning but editorial — not generic salon pink
- **Fonts match:** High-end Houston market aesthetic
