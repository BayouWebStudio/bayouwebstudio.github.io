The Artist

Hi, I'm Britt

Tattoo artist. Storyteller. Your skin is my canvas.

I'm a Houston-based tattoo artist who believes every piece of ink should mean something. Whether it's a bold statement or a delicate detail, I approach each tattoo with care, creativity, and a deep respect for the art form.

I'm currently creating full-time at Revenge Tattoo Studio in Cypress — one of Houston's established studios — where I work with clients who are serious about quality, detail, and the experience of getting tattooed right.

This site is my personal brand — my work, my bookings, my art. When you book with me, you're booking Britt directly.
